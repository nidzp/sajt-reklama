Our Story

From the first ember lit by founder Livia Vespera to the whispered recipes passed down at dawn, every loaf carries three generations of devotion.

We proof dough with patience, let butter rest beneath midnight lights, and weave lavender smoke through the air to welcome neighbors and travelers alike.

Step inside and you will hear the hush of hearth-stones, the low hum of vintage ovens, and the laughter of the Vespera family shaping dawn together.
