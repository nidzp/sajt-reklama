Our Story

Founder Livia Vespera lit the first ember to revive her grandmother’s twilight recipes. Every loaf carries three generations of whispered devotion.

We proof dough with patience, let butter rest beneath midnight lights, and weave lavender smoke through the air to welcome neighbors and travelers alike.

Step inside to hear the hush of hearth-stones, the hum of vintage ovens, and the laughter of the night crew who believes bread tastes best after dusk.
